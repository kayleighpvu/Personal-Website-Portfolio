About Me
I’m Kayleigh Vu, a computer engineer with a passion for full-stack development, UX/UI design, and data systems analytics. I recently earned my B.S. in Computer Engineering with a Mathematics minor from Santa Clara University. Over the past three years, I’ve gained hands-on experience through technical research and internships—working on computer vision systems at Whirlpool and combining web development with data analytics at NVIDIA.

With a strong foundation in both frontend and backend development, I thrive in fast-paced environments where performance and impact matter. I bring a can-do mindset, collaborative energy, and a love for solving real-world problems with technologies.

Outside of work, I’m an active, sports-loving individual—soccer, ultimate frisbee, swimming, snowboarding, and rock climbing are just a few of the ways I stay on my feet (literally!). I also enjoy cars, motorcycles, building PCs, and gaming. No day is ever boring when there’s something new to learn or explore.
